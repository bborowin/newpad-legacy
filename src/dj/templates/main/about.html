Corporate

Newpad is a subsidiary of Yasboti Inc., a Montreal startup dedicated to crafting technologies that simplify the mundane.

Origins

Newpad started as a personal project born out of frustrations of apartment hunting. A proof-of-concept site launched in January 2011; overwhelmingly positive feedback prompted further development.

Awards

In May 2011, the site won first prize in the .CA Impact Awards competition (Web Technology and Design category) and was showcased at the Mesh 2011 conference in Toronto.

Coming up

We're working on some incredible features that will give our users an unfair advantage in the competitive world of apartment hunting. Early-adopter invites available soon!
